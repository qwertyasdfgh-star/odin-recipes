Pork Giniling

Pork Giniling is a pork dish that makes use of ground pork as the primary ingredient. Ground pork is stewed in tomato sauce and water to bring out the taste while vegetables such as carrots and potatoes (some also like this with raisins and green peas) are added for additional flavor and nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo ground pork
  • 1 medium-sized onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, diced
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green peas (optional)
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add ground pork and cook until it turns brown, breaking it apart with a spatula.
  3. Add diced carrots and potatoes. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine all ingredients.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. If using, add green peas and raisins. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Sodium: 800mg
